About the Department
The Department of Computer Technology was established in 2008. The department offers B.Sc (Computer Technology) as three-year undergraduate programme, headed by Dr.K.Mythili , Professor and is followed by 6 Faculty members with nearly 360 students. The department has well qualified, experienced and committed faculty to provide quality industry tuned education to the students. The department conducts many Hands-on Training, Seminars and workshops. Computer Technology Association organizes a National Level Technical Symposium CTECH yearly to exhibit the student talents. The Department is recognized under DBT star College Scheme sponsored by MHRD. The department has very good placement records and many students have been placed in WIPRO Technologies, AMAZON Tech Mahindra, TCS, CTS, INFOSYS, Accenture, VEE technologies, KGISL, Velan InfoTech, Asec Technology, etc.
